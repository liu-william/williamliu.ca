William Liu

Hi there! I'm William, a software developer, and a CS student.

I'm going into my last year of Computing Science at the University of Alberta. From an early age, I was interested in tech and was always intrigued by technological advancements. As such, I'm very optimistic about the future of tech and would love to be a part of it.

Surprisingly, I started university with engineering and ended up not enjoying it. I knew nothing good comes easy in life; therefore, I try my best to be happy along the journey, and as cliche as it sounds, "People are just as happy as they make up their minds to be.". So I knew I needed to take a pivot in my studies and committed to studying CS. At first, I was still hesitant to switch due to my experiences in my engineering programming class. Since there are so many free resources in the developer community, I decided to take a Python class on Coursera and I ended up enjoying it because I finally understood the logic flow of writing programs. Programming is not easy as well. The endless hours of writing out the logic and debugging made it frustrating at times; albeit difficult, I still enjoy programming and love the satisfaction I get from it.

Apart from programming, I enjoy my daily cups of coffee, music, and my sports. I love to watch basketball, European football (soccer), and the Oilers. I occasionally play ball with my buddies at the gym and have been getting into running and spikeball recently.
